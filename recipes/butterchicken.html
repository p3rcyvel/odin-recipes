Butter Chicken

This dish is an ideal make-ahead meal, as it actually gets better when it sits. You can make the recipe all the way through, without garnishing, a whole day before, then simply reheat when youâ€™re ready to eat.

ingredients

  • 1/2 c. plain or Greek yogurt
  • 1 (1") piece ginger, peeled and finely chopped or grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped or grated
  • 2 tsp. garam masala
  • 1 tsp. chili powder
  • 1 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1 tsp. ground turmeric
  • Kosher salt
  • 2 lb. boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1 1/2" cubes
  • 2 tbsp. neutral oil

steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk yogurt, ginger, garlic, garam masala, chili powder, cumin, turmeric, and 2 1/2 teaspoons salt. Stir in chicken and refrigerate at least 1 hour or up to 24.
  2. In a large high-sided skillet over medium-heat heat, heat oil. Add chicken to pan in an even layer and cook, undisturbed, until golden brown on bottom side, 8 to 10 minutes. Using a slotted spoon, transfer chicken to a bowl
